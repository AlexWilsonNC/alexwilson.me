"Poke Battle" is a simulator that factors in various Pokemon's game stats through a third-party API and shows a quick simulation of which creature would win. You can enter a city from anywhere in the world, of which uses that city's real time weather to effect these Pokemon battles as well, which can get real interesting when the original game stats are no longer the only numbers being factored in.

This was a callaborative project with two other classmates of mine. My contributions of which were heavily on the design of the game, seeding and rending the position, effects, and direction of the Pokemon's sprites, as well as JavaScript.
